What would I do to ensure a succesful event?

My plan for the workshop

  • Review the lessons learned in previous conferences and talk with experienced trainers to learn sound practices applied in the past.
  • Check the feedback for the four past conferences and identify the bottlenecks and troubles presented before, during, and after the workshop.
  • Make a tool for tracking the flight numbers of all the participants, their hotel rooms, emergency numbers, and if they require any special medical assistance or must follow a special diet.
  • Prepare any physical training material before the sessions and have a backup plan in case the suppliers cannot deliver them.
